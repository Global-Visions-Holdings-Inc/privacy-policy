Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you are entitled to the following rights:

  1. Right to be Informed: Data subjects have the right to be informed about the collection, processing, and purpose of their data. They should receive clear and transparent information regarding how their data will be used.
  2. Right to Damages: Data subjects have the right to seek indemnification for damages sustained due to inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, false, unlawfully obtained, or unauthorized use of their personal information.
  3. Right to Access: Data subjects have the right to access a copy of their data held by the organization. They should also receive information about the purposes of the processing, methods of processing, recipients of the data, and the period of storage or retention.
  4. Right to File a Complaint: Data subjects have the right to file a complaint if they believe their personal information has been misused, or disclosed improperly, or if any of their data privacy rights have been violated.
  5. Right to Object: Data subjects have the right to object to the processing of their data, particularly in cases where processing is based on consent or legitimate interest.
  6. Right to Rectify: Data subjects have the right to dispute inaccuracies or errors in their data and request corrections or updates to ensure accuracy.
  7. Right to Erasure or Blocking: Data subjects have the right to request the deletion, suspension, or blocking of their personal information under certain circumstances.
  8. Right to Data Portability: Data subjects have the right to receive their data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format for transmission to another data controller.

Note: Certain rights may require further verification or be subject to legal exceptions (e.g., fraud investigation, legal proceedings).

To exercise your rights, you may send a written request to our Data Privacy Office at cop@globalvisions.com.ph. We will respond within the period required by applicable law.
